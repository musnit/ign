Why would you use a liquid layout?

Well you would use a liquid layout when you need to ensure high compatibility with a range of browser sizes and devices. It also gives the user more control of how they see the site and their preferences - so often has better usability. Users with accessibility needs like larger fonts will also likely get a better looking website if a liquid layout is used. You can also use a liquid layout to ensure that there are no horizontal scroll bars, which most people dislike.
